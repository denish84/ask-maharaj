In Gadhada I-1, Maharaj explains that the soul is eternal and pure consciousness, separate from the physical body. It is blissful by nature and its true purpose is to serve God. In Gadhada I-2, Maharaj says that Maya binds the soul through the three gunas - sattva, rajas and tamas. A devotee who has firm faith in God and the Sant can overcome Maya completely. In Sarangpur 1, Maharaj explains the importance of Satsang. He says that without Satsang, liberation is impossible. The company of holy saints purifies the mind and soul.
